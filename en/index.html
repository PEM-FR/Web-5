Web-5 Conference Béziers 2012

The first Web-5 Conference will take place in Béziers, in a department called Hérault (34 <- dpt number).
The conference's point is to let us discover, learn and share new technologies and techniques through a practical approach. Thus allowing developers, sysadmins and technical directors to better grasp how these new technologies and techniques will help them in their daily work (and make their customer's experience even more awesome).
Last but not least, the event will be a good occasion to meet each others, and talk with renown experts about their experience and specialties.

Did I mention the free BeerZ ?

The event will start the 4th of April and end the 6th of April around noon.
The main topics proposed will be : Javascript, Html 5 & CSS 3, WebGL.
The schedule will be announced as soon as our speaker list will be definitive.

We only accept technical talks, no marketing or self promotion will be allowed. Thank you for your understanding.
