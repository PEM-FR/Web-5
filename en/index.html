Web-5 Conference Béziers 2012

Come and be amazed by our marvellous speakers ! They are awesome, they are famous, they roxx, and you've probably always dreamt on meeting one of them ! It is now possible ! You might even end up drinking a beer at the same table kidding at how badass it is to push on prod a friday evening :)

The first Web-5 Conference will take place in Béziers, in a department called Hérault (34 <- dpt number) the 4th, 5th, and 6th of April 2012.
The conference's point is to let us discover, learn and share new technologies and techniques through a practical approach. Thus allowing developers, sysadmins and technical directors to better grasp how these new technologies and techniques will help them in their daily work (and make their customer's experience even more awesome).


Note that there is a ryanair airport in Béziers with flights to the UK, Sweden, Norway, Germany and more :)
