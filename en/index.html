Sun, Grapes & Web-5

1st EDITION : April 2012 in Béziers(34) - France

Web-5 Conference JavaScript, Html5, Dojo, WebGL, Mobile, Performance


Come and be amazed by our marvellous speakers! They are awesome, they are famous, they roxx, and you've probably always dreamt on meeting one of them! It is now possible! You might even end up drinking a beer at the same table kidding about how badass it is to push on prod a friday evening :)
We believe the schedule speaks for itself... Take this unique chance to see the real thing live, and learn a lot attending their talks!

The first Web-5 Conference will take place in Béziers, in a department called Hérault (34 <- dpt number) the 4th, 5th, and 6th of April 2012.
The conference's point is to let us discover, learn and share new technologies (WebGL, Dojo Toolkit, SocketIO, NodeJS, HTML5, JavaScript) and techniques through a practical approach. Thus allowing developers, sysadmins and technical directors to better grasp how these new technologies and techniques will help them in their daily work (and make their customer's experience even more awesome).


Note that there is a ryanair airport in Béziers with flights to the UK, Sweden, Norway, Germany and more :)
You can also check Montpellier, Carcassones or Toulouse for airports. If you fly to Paris, you can take the train to Béziers from Paris, it takes about 4 hours max, or take a plane from Beauvais (Paris) ryanair to Béziers!

Last but not least, the event will be a good occasion to meet each others, and talk with renowned experts about their experience and specialties.

Did I mention the free BeerZ?

The event will start the 4th of April and end the 6th of April around 18h00 (6h00pm).
The main topics proposed will be : Javascript, Dojo Toolkit, Node.js, Html 5 & CSS 3, WebGL and Scala.
